American NASCAR driver Kyle Busch has an estimated net worth of $40 million in 2012. Currently participating in the Sprint Cup Series, Kyle Busch is driving No. 18 Mars, Inc./Interstate Batteries Toyota Camry for Joe Gibbs Racing. This racer is the owner of Kyle Busch Motorsports.

With his 26 wins, Kyle Busch currently holds the record of most number of wins in a single season. He was also the first NASCAR driver to have won the top three touring series of NASCAR. After surpassing the record of Mark Martin who had 49 wins against his 54 wins, Kyle Busch is the current title holder of most number of wins in a NASCAR Nationwide series.

Kyle Busch won the Richmond International Raceway and the 2009 Crown Royal Presents the Russell Friedman 400. He is the younger brother of Kurt Busch, the Sprint Cup series champion in 2004.

Kyle Thomas Busch was born on May 2, 1985,  in Las Vegas, Nevada, U.S.A. Since December 2010, Kyle Busch has been married to Samantha Sarcinella.
