Who is Bianca Kajlich? Why is she famous?

Bianca Kajlich is a famous American actress famous for her role of the main character in NBC sitcom Undateable and Christina Henry in the Amazon Prime series Bosch. She was born in  Seattle, Washington, on 26 March 1977.

Bianca is the daughter of Patti (née Campana) and Dr. Aurel Jan “Relo” Kajlich. Her father is of Slovakian descent and her mother is of Italian descent. 

Bianca graduated from the Bishop Blanchet High School in Seattle, Washington, and later studied in the famous Washington State University in Pullman, Washington.
