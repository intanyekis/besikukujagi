Ariana Grande Will Share Details Of Her Affair With Ethan Slater In New Music After Backlash

Highlights

  • Ariana Grande's new music will reflect her personal life, including relationships with Dalton Gomez and Ethan Slater.
  • Ariana aims to provide insight for fans through her upcoming album "Eternal Sunshine."
  • The singer admits that her new album will be emotional and raw, with unspecified release details.

There’s been concern that Ariana Grande’s alleged affair with Ethan Slater could ruin her career, as fans were horrified when the Wicked co-stars debuted their relationship while still married to other people.

Ethan’s wife, who had just welcomed a baby, furthered cheating speculation when she said Ariana isn’t a “girl’s girl” to the media, while she and Ethan have moved forward with their divorce. Ariana has also finalized her divorce from Dalton Gomez following two years of marriage.

Ariana Wants Fans To Understand Where She’s Coming From

Now that the dust appears to have settled, Ariana reportedly wants to tell her side of the story, and one report says she’s going to use new music as an outlet.

“Ariana thinks these songs will provide a lot of insight for her fans,” a source told Us Weekly about Ariana’s upcoming album Eternal Sunshine. “[It’s] her side of the story regarding Dalton [Gomez] and Ethan [Slater].”

This isn’t the first hint fans have gotten that Ariana’s new music, which is expected to release later this year, will refer to her personal life. Promoting her latest single, also called “Enternal Sunshine,” in February, Ariana said she hopes her fans understand more about her through new lyrics.

“I feel like we don’t need to go into any specifics, but, of course, there’s like an insatiable frustration, inexplicable, hellish feeling with watching people misunderstand the people you love and you and anything,” she said while appearing on the “Zach Sang Show.” “I’m so nervous because pieces of [the album] touch on things that are real and then pieces of it are also just, like, part of the concept.”

The former Nickelodeon star continued, “So, what is that separation? It’s so scary to leave it up to these selective memory people to decipher. It’s scary, but I digress. Too late. The vinyls have been printed.”

Ariana Admits Her New Album Will Be “Emotional”

While Ethan and Ariana debuted their romance in July, some fans wondered if things fizzled out quickly, as the couple seemingly put things on pause following the backlash. But within a few months, not only were the pair referring to each other on social media, but one report said Ethan had moved into Ariana’s place as he navigates his divorce.

While specifics aren’t known about Ariana’s new album, including its release date, the singer has teased that it’s going to be very “emotional” and raw compared to her other releases.

“It says everything and nothing at the same time. It’s very vague and very specific — all at once,” she added on the “Zach Sang Show.”

“The thing that was beautiful about it was, when I was writing [Eternal Sunshine], it was with no intention for the world to hear it. I was just kind of [like], ‘Let’s just go. Let’s see what comes out,” the Victorious alum went on. “And then, when I was done, I remembered that people will hear it and sometimes, people will sensationalize things and assign meaning to certain things. And I wanted to make sure that I combed through, just to make sure that it was what I intended.”
