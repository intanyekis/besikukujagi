Kanye West Puts New Wife In Charge Of His Failing Businesses

Kanye West’s latest marriage may not be legal, but he’s already given the reigns of his multiple businesses to his new wife, Bianca Censori.

According to a new report, the architect has stepped in to help manages Kanye’s business ventures amid his various legal and financial problems, and Ye hasn’t been hesitant to let Bianca take control.

"Bianca runs the show. She has been around Ye for about three years, things got serious really fast in mid-November,” a source told The Sun.

"Bianca handles Ye's daily logistics from Yeezy clothing management to construction of the new Yeezy headquarters/ Donda Academy,” they continued. "She takes all his calls, and all business affairs go through her. It seems to be working out well so far."

However, this is reportedly worrying those close to Kanye. The same source alleges people are skeptical of Bianca’s motivations.

Kanye and Bianca weren’t publicly linked until after they tied the knot in January 2023. Though the pair reportedly held a wedding ceremony and consider their union to be legitimate, they’ve yet to file for a wedding license.

They reportedly met through Kanye’s Yeezy brand, which Bianca started working for in 2020.

Both Kanye and Bianca are facing a multitude of challenges with his businesses. Ye lost this billionaire status after Adidas cancelled their Yeezy collaboration following the rapper’s anti-semitic and anti-Black comments.

Yeezy has been floundering since then. Not only have sales tanked, but it was reported late last year that the brand’s headquarters were facing eviction over unpaid rent.

Yeezy also owes $600,000 in tax debt , while Kanye reportedly owes $50 million to the IRS and had his bank accounts frozen over it.

Moreover, Kanye is facing a number of lawsuits. His former business manager filed a $4.5 million case for unpaid wages and breach of contract after Ye allegedly fired him without cause.

He’s also had to make a number of undisclosed settlements to ex-employees over toxic workplace and discrimination allegations. Other lawsuits Kanye is facing include a $250 million defamation case filed by George Floyd’s family over untrue comments the Yeezy founder made about George’s death.

More recently, Kanye West’s attorneys officially served him notice that they were quitting. The firm reportedly had a hard time tracking Ye down, as he stopped replying to messages and wasn’t staying at his usual addresses.

Needless to say, Bianca has a lot on her hands if she’s truly taking charge of Kanye’s businesses.
