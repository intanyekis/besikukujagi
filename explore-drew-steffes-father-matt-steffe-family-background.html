Drew Steffe’s father: Matt Steffe, an accomplished college basketball player, has passed on his passion for the sport to his son, Drew. Hailing from a family with a deep-rooted love for basketball, Drew has been embraced by Texas supporters for his exceptional skills and dedication. Age of Drew Steffe’s Father: Matt Steffe

Matt Steffe, aged 48, is a renowned former college basketball player and coach. Notably, he began his coaching career at the age of 24, defying the norm of starting at a later stage in life. With over two decades of experience, Matt is a highly esteemed figure in the basketball community.

Matt Steffe’s Background

Matt grew up in Southern California’s San Fernando Valley, showing an early aptitude for basketball. His exceptional skills led him to receive multiple accolades during his time at Hart High School in California. With a full NCAA Division I scholarship, he attended the Air Force Academy in the WAC Conference and later went on to play professionally in Croatia. Following this, he transitioned into coaching, making a name for himself as the Assistant Coach of Quincy at the age of 24. Matt’s dedication to the sport led him to establish ‘D1 Basketball DFW’ in 2013, a program aimed at nurturing upcoming basketball talents.

A family man, Matt is married to Beth, a former soccer star from Quincy, and together they have three children: Drew, Ava, and Dylan. The Steffe family is highly passionate about sports and often shares glimpses of their fun-filled moments on social media.
