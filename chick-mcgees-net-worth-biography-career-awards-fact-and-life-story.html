Chick McGees Net Worth, Biography, Career, Awards, Fact and Life Story

Net Worth:$3 Million

Chick McGee Net Worth: Chick McGee is an American radio personality who has a net worth of $3 million. Chick McGee was born in London, Ohio in November 1957. He is best known for being part of The Bob & Tom Show. He chose the pseudonym Chick McGee based on the character Jack McGee from The Incredible Hulk.

He graduated from an independent broadcast school in 1976. His career started in West Virginia and Ohio. Chick joined The Bob & Tom Show in April 1986. McGee has acted as announce, a sports commentator, and comic foil for the show. He played the role of Mr. Obvious and started a tradition of getting a prostate exam on air to raise awareness for prostate cancer. McGee left the show in January 1995 but returned in July of the same year. He started to podcast in July 2012 with his now former girlfriend Jessica Hooker and producer Jason Hoffsetz. McGee appeared in the 2010 film The Big Idea and in episodes of The Bob & Tom Show TV series, which he also wrote for.
