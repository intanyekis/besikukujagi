Ric Flair Reminisces About Driving &amp; Partying With Andre The Giant

was more “Driving For Andre” than “Dinner With Andre.” In a recent episode of Soup With Coop starring Cooper Manning, Flair talked about how he and Andre The Giant’s careers coincided in the same year and how they eventually crossed paths not just in the locker room, but on the road.

“Andre started in Montreal in ’72. Same time I started,” Flair tells Cooper. “He came to Minneapolis to complete his training while I was there and Verne Gagne, the promoter and the guy that broke me in had me drive Andre around like to Chicago and Milwaukee from Minneapolis any marquee event he was at, usually like the big cities. And so I just drove Andre down, and then like in Chicago (they didn’t put this in the HBO special) I would take him to Rush Street, man it was a great time. He was a character. Every woman alive is fascinated by a 7’4″ man (laughs).”

I wasn’t ‘The Nature Boy,’ I was just a fat boy then, but heh, if I had ‘The Nature Boy’ going on back we would have had a lot more fun.”
