Forum Software and Features

Warriorsworld Forum is powered by the open-source forum software called KAWF. Developed by a community of dedicated programmers, KAWF offers a robust and customizable platform for online discussions and community engagement.

Unlike some other forums, Warriorsworld does not have collapsed threaded discussions enabled by default. However, users have the flexibility to customize their preferences and enable this feature if desired. This allows for more organized and focused conversations within specific threads.

One of the key design principles of Warriorsworld Forum is to prioritize community engagement. The forum’s layout and features are carefully designed to encourage meaningful interactions and active participation. Individual conversations are given prominence, making it easy for users to follow discussions and identify participants.

In order to maintain a clean and distraction-free environment, Warriorsworld Forum does not incorporate emoticons, logos, avatars, or other flashy elements. The focus remains on the content and the discussions happening within the community.

Moderation and Posting Guidelines

Warriorsworld Forum maintains a minimal approach to forum moderation, ensuring that posts in the main forum are marked as off-topic only if they do not involve sports. We also discourage the use of excessive foul language, as the forum is accessible from work environments.

A team of dedicated moderators oversees the main forum, with select members granted specific privileges to ensure effective moderation. These moderators work diligently to maintain a positive and respectful atmosphere within the forum.

At Warriorsworld Forum, we have comprehensive guidelines in place for content posting. We prohibit the use of complete articles or content from other sites without proper attribution, reinforcing our commitment to originality and respect for intellectual property.

Link posting is allowed, but we emphasize the need to avoid spamming and self-promotion. Our goal is to ensure that all posted links contribute value to the discussions and enhance the overall user experience.

“At Warriorsworld Forum, we believe in fostering a community that respects both the content being posted and the individuals engaged in discussions.”

Examples of Warriorsworld Forum Guidelines:

  • Respect copyright laws and give proper attribution when sharing content from other sources.

  • Contribute meaningful links relevant to the discussion topics without engaging in spamming or self-promotion.
  • Report any incidences of inappropriate behavior or violations of the forum guidelines to the moderators.

By adhering to these moderation and posting guidelines, Warriorsworld Forum aims to cultivate a respectful and engaging community where members can freely share their insights and opinions about the Golden State Warriors and basketball.

Ticket Sales and Selling Guidelines

When it comes to ticket sales, Warriorsworld Forum upholds guidelines that prioritize fair and transparent transactions among its members. The forum encourages users to sell tickets at face value or even below, ensuring that fellow Warriors fans are not subjected to inflated prices or unfair deals.

We believe in fostering a sense of camaraderie among the Warriors community, and that includes treating each other with respect and fairness in all ticket transactions. By adhering to these selling guidelines, we aim to create a positive and supportive environment within the forum.

Here are some key points to keep in mind:

  • Sell tickets at face value or below: We encourage users to offer tickets at the original face value or even at a discounted price. This ensures that Warriors fans have access to tickets without having to pay exorbitant prices.
  • Discourage gouging fellow fans: We strongly discourage any attempts to exploit fellow Warriors fans by charging excessively high prices for tickets. We want to prioritize the affordability and accessibility of tickets for all members.
  • Avoid selling to fans of opposing teams: While we respect and welcome fans of all NBA teams, it is recommended to prioritize selling tickets to fellow Warriors supporters. This helps create a more vibrant and unified fan experience during games, particularly when playing against rival teams like the Kings or Lakers.

    Formatting Messages

    When participating in the Warriorsworld Forum, users have the option to format their text using various HTML tags, allowing for enhanced visual appeal and emphasis on specific elements. Here are some of the formatting options available:

    • Bold: Users can make text bold by enclosing it within the <strong> or <b> tags.
    • Italic: Text can be emphasized with italics by using the <em> or <i> tags.
    • Underline: Inderlined text can be achieved by wrapping it with the <u> or <ins> tags.
    • Changing Font Color: Users can apply different font colors to their text by using the <font color=”color”> tags, where “color” represents the desired color code.
    • Superscript: To create a superscript effect, use the <sup> tag. For example, H2O for water.
    • Subscript: To create a subscript effect, use the <sub> tag. For example, CO2 for carbon dioxide.

    By utilizing these HTML tags, users can customize their messages, making them visually engaging and effectively conveying their thoughts within the Warriorsworld community.

    Here’s an example of how users can format their messages using HTML tags:
