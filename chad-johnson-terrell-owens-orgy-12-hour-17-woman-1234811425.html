Chad Johnson And Terrell Owens Reminisce On 12-Hour, 17-Woman Orgy

Chad “Ochocinco” Johnson and Terrell Owens shared a few laughs on social media this week as they reminisced on a shared sexual experience.

During the livestream conversation, Johnson begins by asking Owens if he remembers “that time we was in the DR with them women. We had a lil orgy.”

“Aw yeah, I had the big girl,” Owens replied in a clip captured by TNT. “I had to take one for the team…big girls need love too!”

Johnson continued to recall the evening, sharing, “We went though about 17 women in, what, 12 hours?”

“Was it 17?” Owens questioned in a high-pitched voice, leading Johnson to repeat himself.

“My mind ain’t good dog…17? Man I thought it was about 12,” Owens questioned again before adding, “I was done after about two or three! I had to recuperate, I had to recover, dog!”

The two NFL veterans played together on the 2010 Bengals roster.

The conversation comes weeks after Owens was struck by a vehicle following a dispute with a man over a basketball game.

The Oct. 16 incident stemmed from a heated exchange between Owens and a man during a pick-up game in Calabasas, Calif. earlier that evening. Owens was then hit in his knee by a car driven by the same man as the football pro was preparing to leave.

The athlete didn’t sustain any serious injuries and did not receive medical attention, according to police. No arrests have been made in the case.
