See Margot Robbie &amp; More Hollywood Life

The highly anticipated release of ‘Asteroid City’ is on the horizon! And it has a little something for everyone — the ‘science fiction romantic comedy drama’ flick from iconic filmmaker Wes Anderson features an eclectic ensemble cast including Scarlett Johansson, Jason Schwartzman, Oscar winner Tom Hanks, Tilda Swinton, ‘Breaking Bad’ star Bryan Cranston, Edward Norton, Adrien Brody, and Liev Schreiber, among many others. The film is expected to delight audiences — its highly acclaimed director is responsible for cult classics including ‘The Royal Tenenbaums’ and ‘The Fantastic Mr. Fox,’ among others. 

On Tuesday, Jun 13, the illustrious cast and supporters of the film lined up for the summer premiere in New York City. Here, ‘Black Widow’ beauty and mother of two Scarlett Johansson positively glows in a white mermaid style dress with a floral accent and spagetti string tie. The wife of Colin Jost, of course, brough old Hollywood glam with her curly, mid-length blonde locks, and perfected the entire look with neutral pink makeup tones. 

Along with Scarlett, ‘Barbie’ Movie star Margot Robbie attended the event, with a bevy of other stars and industry professionals. Click through to see pics of the guests at the big NYC premiere here!
