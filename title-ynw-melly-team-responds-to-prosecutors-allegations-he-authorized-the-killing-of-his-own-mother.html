YNW Melly Accused Of Authorizing The Killing Of His Mother

Broward County, FL - 

YNW Melly has been behind bars since February 2019 when he was arrested for the deaths of fellow crew members YNW Sakchaser and YNW Juvy. The State of Florida is reportedly seeking the death penalty for the crime, while Melly continues to proclaim his innocence.

But on Sunday (June 26), No Jumper shared an Instagram post with alleged paperwork tying YNW Melly to another outrageous allegation. According to the document, Melly allegedly green-lit the potential murder of his mother.

When asked if prosecutors thought Melly commissioned any crimes, the response was, “Yes. If you read his phone, there’s narcotics trafficking and firearms violations authorizing the killing of his mother. Those are the things that you would consider ‘authorizing.’”

As the post began to gain traction, a PSA showed up on YNW Melly’s Instagram Stories that read, “LIES. FALSE AGENDA. INNOCENT!!” Whatever the case, prosecutors are confident they have a slam dunk.

AD LOADING...

Detective Christopher Williams will be called to the stand as an expert witness during the forthcoming trial, where he’ll testify it’s highly probable Melly was the triggerman — not co-defendant Cortlen Henry. During the initial investigation into the murders, authorities determined Melly shot the two men while Henry staged the crime to appear like a drive-by shooting.
