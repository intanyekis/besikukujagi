Emily Ratajkowski  - Career

Emily Ratajkowski Career: - Emily Ratajkowski started her career as a child actress who first appeared in a nickelodeon series. Later Ratajkowski met and signed with a talent agency after being encouraged by an acting teacher. That same day, at the age of 14, she signed up with Ford and went on to perform teen print catalog modeling for Kohl's and Nordstrom. Ratajkowski starred in the 2013 video "Blurred Lines" by Robin Thicke, T.I., and Pharrell Williams. Ratajkowski has previously been in two other music videos: Taio Cruz's "Fast Car," which was published on November 5, 2012, and Maroon 5's "Love Someone," which was released two months after "Blurred Lines." Ben Affleck chose her to portray the mistress of his character in David Fincher's 2014 adaptation of Gillian Flynn's novel Gone Girl after seeing her in the "Blurred Lines" video.  Ratajkowski's portrayal was characterized as "nuanced" by the U-T San Diego, while Andrew O'Hehir of Salon and Wesley Morris of Grantland both noted that her minor role as a "duplicitous and manipulative former pupil" was critical. Ratajkowski was selected as the face of Versace's Spring/Summer 2023 campaign in January 2023.
