Chrissie Fit See Pics Of The Pitch Perfect 2 Star Hollywood Life

Chrissie Fit, born April 3, 1984, is an actress and singer. Her big break came in 2007 when she starred as Mercedes / Mercedes Juarez on the popular soap opera ‘General Hospital.’ From there, Chrissie earned the role of Flo in the movie ‘Pitch Perfect 2,’ where she got to show off her singing talent. 

She reprised the role in the ‘Pitch Perfect’ series third installment in 2017. Between projects, she also starred on the Disney Channel Original Movie ‘Teen Beach Movie’ as CheeChee. She reprised the role in the 2015 sequel. In 2020, Chrissie appeared on the series ‘Awkwafina Is Nora from Queens.’ She has a number of projects in the works.
