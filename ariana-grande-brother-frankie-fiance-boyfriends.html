The Complicated Truth About Ariana Grandes Brother Frankies Relationship History

Ariana Grande is one of the most successful and famous singers in the world. However, not everyone knows about her older brother, Frankie. Just as Ariana married her Realtor boyfriend back in May of 2021, her brother is also engaged and planning a wedding.

Frankie Grande proposed to her boyfriend, Hale Leon, in June of 2021. Since then, he seems incredibly happy that he's found his forever person. However, this isn't the first time that Ariana Grande's older brother's love life has been in the public eye. Before meeting his current fiance and fellow actor, Hale, Frankie was in a throuple relationship with another married couple. Let's take a look at some of his past relationships to see how far he's come.

Frankie Grande Was in a Relationship With a Married Couple

Before meeting the love of his life, Frankie was dating a married couple, meaning all three people were in a committed relationship with each other. Grande told Us Weekly, "Being in a throuple was so complicated…you have double the highs, double the excitement…but also double the lows, double the drama."
