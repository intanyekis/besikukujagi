Kourtney Kardashian &amp; Younes Bendjimas Bonnie &amp; Clyde Costumes 2017 Hollywood Life

Kourtney Kardashian, 38, and her boyfriend, Younes Bendjima, 24, may not be thieves in real life, but their take on Bonnie and Clyde certainly stole the title for best couple’s Halloween costume on Oct. 31. To create her look, Kourt wore an orange cropped top and a tweed slit skirt. She accessorized with a matching scarf, beige beret, and a pair of pointed oxfords. But the best part of the entire ensemble is actually the blonde wig which totally proved once and for all that every Kardashian can pull off lighter locks. Younes played his part by wearing a blue pinstripe suit paired with a printed tie and a fedora. Check out their sleek Halloween style below!

While the couple looked positively perfect for the occasion, the Keeping Up With The Kardashian star’s outfit didn’t fully cooperate. While stepping out of the car, the thigh-high slit on her skirt unfortunately moved in a way that wasn’t ideal. It ended up revealing part of Kourt’s underwear right as paparazzi were snapping pics of the star and one of the photographers caught a clear shot of her crotch. While this surely isn’t the best experience, the mom of three is a pro and didn’t let the little wardrobe malfunction phase her. And why should it? She looked great! Click here to see pics of her and Younes.

This wasn’t the only time this year that the reality TV star was spotted in a matching costume. Over the weekend, she teamed up with her sister,  Kim Kardashian, 37, to pull off the greatest Michael Jackson and Madonna costumes we’ve ever seen. Then she donned a Tin Man costume to recreate the Wizard Of Oz with her children earlier on Halloween night. She even shared the cutest photo on Instagram of Penelope Disick trick-or-treating as Dorothy. Noticeably absent from the family fun though, was ex Scott Disick, who didn’t post anything from the holiday at all. We aren’t completely surprised though.

A source told HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY that Scott tried to invite his girlfriend Sofia Richie, 19, along for the night’s festivities, and Kourtney wasn’t having it. Hey, there’s always next year!
