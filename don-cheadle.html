    Co-founder of Not On Our Watch

    The 55 years old actor, Don is also the co-founder of Not On Out Watch organization. The organization is a non-governmental, international relief, and humanitarian aid foundation. Well, he founded this organization along with several other A-listed actors such as George Clooney, Matt Damon, Brad Pitt, David Pressman, and Jerry Weintraub.

    Not On Our Watch was established in 2008 to bring global attention to human rights violations in Darfur. They provide resources to help in putting an end to mass atrocities. The organization emerges from the book Not On Our Watch, which was written by Don and human rights activist John Prendergast.
