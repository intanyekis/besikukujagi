Slaughterhouse Announces New 2014 Glasshouse Tour

Beginning in March 2014, the men of Slaughterhouse will begin their Glass House North American tour. Joell Ortiz, Crooked I, Royce Da 5’9 and Joe Budden will kick off their tour during a 5-day stint at the SXSW Festival then work their way through the Americas and Canada until April 26. Tickets are available now at Glasshousetour.com.
