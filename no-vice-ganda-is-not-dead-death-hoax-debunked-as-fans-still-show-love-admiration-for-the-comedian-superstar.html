No, Vice Ganda Is Not Dead Death Hoax Debunked As Fans Still Show Love &amp; Admiration For The Come

Vice Ganda is a well-known Filipino comedian, talk show presenter, and actor hosting the ABS-CBN show “It’s Showtime.”

Vice Ganda began his career as a vocalist, then as a comic for Punchline and The Library, where he was given the stage name “Vice Ganda” by the owner, Andrew de Real.

Is Vice Ganda Dead? Death Hoax Debunnked

Vice Ganda has made fewer public appearances recently, leading some to speculate if he is dead or alive at this time. He is, without a doubt, still alive and doing well these days.

Ganda also recently posted images on Instagram, indicating that he is not dead and all those rumors about him are not true.

Due to a health condition, Vice could not participate in “It’s Showtime” for three days in 2019. During that period, he was hospitalized. Later, he expressed gratitude to all of his well-wishers for their love and support.

Is Vice Ganda Listed On Wikipedia?

Vice Ganda’s biography is listed on the official page of Wikipedia, and it includes information regarding his early life, career, and personal life.

Ganda won the Push award for most popular social media figure in particular. He also got the 2021 innovative award, and the Eduk Circle Island Dekada Awards named him the most influential celebrity of the decade in 2020.

He’s also published three studio albums, including Lakas Tama, on which he also debuted his novel, President Vice, on July 1, 2016.

Vice Ganda has starred in several box office successes in the Philippines, all of which have grossed a lot of money.

Ganda has appeared in several films, including Familia Blondina, Fantastica, Your Face Sounds Familiar, and Search For The Idol Philippines, to name a few.

Vice Ganda Personal Life Details

Vice Ganda, who identifies as non-binary, doesn’t mind being addressed with either masculine or feminine pronouns.

He has been dating Ion Perez, a member of the supporting cast of “It’s Showtime,” since November 2019. Benigno Dungo Perez, better known by his stage name Ion Perez, is a Filipino actor, model, and TV personality.
